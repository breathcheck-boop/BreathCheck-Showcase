Install and Access

Install as PWA (Desktop or Mobile)

  1. Open the BreathCheck app in a modern browser.
  2. Use browser “Install app” or “Add to Home Screen”.
  3. Launch from desktop/home screen like a regular app.

After first load and cache warm-up, the app shell and core routes can still open offline.

Use in Browser

  • No installer package is required.
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.
  • Each browser/device keeps its own local data.

BreathCheck is local-first. Sensitive entries are encrypted locally before persistence.

6-Module Program

Modules unlock in sequence to keep progress clear and manageable.

1. Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety basics, body signals, and the worry cycle.

2. Relaxation

Grounding skills and breathing-based calming practice.

3. Our Thoughts

Identify cognitive distortions and anxious thought patterns.

4. Changing Thoughts

Challenge and reframe automatic anxious thinking.

5. Coping with Worry

Use scheduled worry-time and refocus routines.

6. Lifestyle Factors

Sleep, movement, mindfulness, and relapse prevention planning.

Practical Tools

Breath Break

Privacy and Safety

Local-first and encrypted

BreathCheck stores data locally in each browser/device. Sensitive entries are encrypted locally before they are saved.

Supportive, not emergency care

BreathCheck is for self-help and skills practice. It is not a crisis or emergency response service.
